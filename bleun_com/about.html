About us
Who are we


Since 1973, our today's Chief developer Dietmar Loewe works in the field of infrared technology. Here, he has created many fundamentals that are applied today in a big way.
From about 2003, the first three cooperation partners worked under the brand name "Bleun" (there are the initial letters of the names of the founders).
Later was added the name on "Bleun Energiesysteme", because in this time also heating plates were manufactured and sold.
Today the basic virtues provided first again by "Bleun" to the: research and development. This is reflected by "Engineering" in the name, which also has expressed, that the developer cooperative has greatly internationalized.
The composition of the staff varies very strongly depending on the need and requirement. Only Dietmar Loewe is an exception and is a constant in the cooperative with his great experience.
