Generators in combination with magnetic motors and electric motors
An ongoing Issue

Entry

As soon as a project has involved so much commitment and capital as in "the development of effective energy production and its use or its transformation into other forms of energy, with the inclusion of natural existing free energies".
This included the research and development of magnet generators and motors, in combination with generators and electric motors, to improve the efficiency of drives, but mainly for electric motors.
Many attempts have led to our findings (below some pictures) by which we can conclude which future projects will bring success.
