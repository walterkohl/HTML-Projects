About my impressions

Since the 70's photo collages have been a creative expression of my cultural- and social and body-historical interests.
Between 1976 and 2010 I participated in group exhibitions on photography in Germany and the Netherlands. So in Cologne, Aachen, Erfurt, Weimar, Maastricht, Utrecht, Bonn, Frankfurt and Karlsruhe. The photographic encyclopaedias by Muybridge, Eakins, ... were stimulating and exemplary. Marey, the body photographies of the 20s (Koppitz, Drtikol, Riebicke, Bayer, ...), the new Body pictures of the 80s by Ritts, Mapplethorpe, D. or G. Blum, Minkkinen, ... and finally, Claudia Rogge, for example. All in all - a magic of seriel pictures or serial duplications.
